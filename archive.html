A PATHWAY TO PROGRESS

an EasyGov Brochure

Global efforts to recover from COVID19 are underway; however, it is essential to look at the numbers to realize how to recover best. With a rise in the economic and social divide, especially in emerging and low-income developing countries, the solution does not lie in welfare budget increases; it lies in a transformation of the welfare design. ‘A Pathway towards Progressive Social Welfare’ by EasyGov gives an insight into the world post-pandemic, identifying the 'need of the hour' to safeguard the interests of people and governments alike.
